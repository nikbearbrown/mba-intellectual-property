# Introduction to Intellectual Property: with LLMs
## Compact Tik TOC Architecture

**Book type:** MBA / business law elective text  
**Status:** Retrospective architecture for developed content  

## Core Thesis

This book teaches intellectual property as a business strategy and risk framework: patents, copyrights, trademarks, trade secrets, licensing, enforcement, and AI-era ownership questions.

## Primary Reader

MBA students, founders, product managers, creators, and professionals who need IP literacy.

## Learning Arc

1. **IP foundations:** what each IP regime protects and why.
2. **Business decisions:** licensing, branding, innovation, secrecy, enforcement, and valuation.
3. **AI-era complications:** generated content, training data, ownership, disclosure, and risk management.

## Chapter Architecture

Each chapter should begin with a business asset or dispute, then map it to the relevant IP regime and strategic choice.

## Terminal Capability

The reader can identify IP issues, ask informed counsel questions, and design basic IP-aware business practices.

## Adoption Risks

- Must avoid legal advice.
- AI/IP law changes rapidly.
- Jurisdictional differences need clear caveats.
